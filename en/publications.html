Publications

A research-centre-style publications page with filters by year, journal ranking, research area, and search by title, authors, or journal. The CAPES/Periódicos-ADM 2025–2028 classification is used as a complementary source when a more specific indicator is not available. The initial dataset prioritizes papers from the last five years in journals indexed in Scopus, Web of Science, or Spell, while the database can be expanded automatically from the Lattes CV.

Lattes integration: update data/publications_lse_nber.json manually or by script. Colored icons indicate relative impact: green for higher impact, yellow for intermediate impact, and gray when the classification still needs confirmation. Google Scholar links are generated automatically from each paper title.
