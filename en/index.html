UFES · Research group

Accounting, auditing, taxation, and public finance with academic rigor and applied impact.

GECAT conducts scientific research in accounting, auditing, taxation, public finance, macroeconomics, and applied econometrics, producing evidence to advance academic knowledge and support better institutional and economic decisions.

Mission

Scientific research and applied professional knowledge

GECAT produces rigorous and relevant research connecting accounting, governance, fiscal policy, public transparency, and markets. The group also develops applied consulting, expert analysis, and technical advisory projects.
