Coordination

Academic profile

Associate Professor III at the Federal University of Espírito Santo (UFES), working in undergraduate programs and in the Graduate Program in Accounting (PPGCON/UFES). He holds a degree in Accounting from PUC Minas, a master's degree in Accounting and a PhD in Administration, with emphasis in Finance, from UFMG.

He also holds specializations in Business Management from IBMEC/MG and in Data Science and Analytics from USP/ESALQ. He served two terms as coordinator of PPGCON/UFES, is a member of FAPES advisory committees, a FAPES Research Fellow, and a member of the Academia Mineira de Ciências Contábeis.

His scientific output includes articles in qualified journals, papers presented at national and international conferences, and academic awards. He has served in editorial roles for scientific journals and participates in academic committees of conferences such as ANPCONT, SEMEAD and ENANPAD.

His research interests include IFRS, accounting information quality, valuation, taxation, auditing and corporate governance, focusing on the effects of financial reporting and disclosure on economic decisions, regulation and markets.
